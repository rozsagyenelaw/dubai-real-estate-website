About BAYZ 101

Presenting the most luxurious project in the Danube Properties portfolio - BAYZ 101. Rising majestically as a 101-level architectural masterpiece in the heart of Business Bay, this iconic tower redefines luxury living in Dubai. Just steps away from Business Bay Metro Station, BAYZ 101 offers unparalleled connectivity to Dubai's most coveted destinations including Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, and the Museum of the Future.

With over 40 luxurious amenities spread across multiple levels, BAYZ 101 caters to the most discerning residents. From the infinity pool overlooking Dubai's iconic skyline to private pools in select apartments, from a state-of-the-art bowling center to a golf simulator, every detail has been crafted for an extraordinary lifestyle. The flexible 80-month payment plan with just 1% monthly payments makes this prestigious address accessible to investors and homebuyers alike.
