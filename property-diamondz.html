About Diamondz

Presenting Diamondz by Danube Properties - ultra luxury living in the heart of Jumeirah Lake Towers (JLT). This premium residential tower offers fully furnished apartments with stunning views of Jumeirah Island, DMCC Tower, and Dubai Marina. Located in the Uptown District of JLT near Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Road, Diamondz provides the perfect blend of luxury, convenience, and accessibility.

Diamondz features studio, 1, 2, 3, and 4-bedroom apartments ranging from 407 to 1,973 sq.ft., all fully furnished to the highest standards. Select apartments include private pools for the ultimate luxury experience. With world-class amenities including rooftop pool bar, spa, state-of-the-art gym, prayer hall, and kids facilities, Diamondz caters to every lifestyle need. The flexible 70:30 payment plan with 1% monthly installments makes your dream home accessible. Completion is expected by November 2027.
