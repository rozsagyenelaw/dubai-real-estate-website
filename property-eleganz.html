About Eleganz

Welcome to Eleganz - a beautifully completed residential development in the heart of Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC). This stunning community offers ready-to-move-in apartments and townhouses designed for modern luxury living. Located in one of Dubai's most sought-after family-friendly neighborhoods, Eleganz combines convenience with elegance.

Eleganz features thoughtfully designed 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, duplex apartments, and spacious townhouses with premium finishes and contemporary architecture. All units feature high-quality fixtures, modern kitchens, and spacious layouts perfect for comfortable living. The project is fully completed and ready for immediate occupancy, making it ideal for both investors and end-users seeking move-in-ready homes.
