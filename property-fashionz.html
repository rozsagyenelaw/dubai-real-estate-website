About Fashionz

Presenting BAYZ 102 - a soaring 102-level architectural masterpiece by Danube Properties in the heart of Business Bay. This iconic tower redefines luxury living in Dubai, offering unparalleled connectivity to the city's most coveted destinations. Located just minutes from Business Bay Metro Station, Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, and the Museum of the Future.

With 54 luxurious amenities spread across multiple zones - Water Zone, Activity Zone, Family Zone, and Facility Zone - BAYZ 102 caters to the most discerning residents. Experience the Skyline Pool at Lounge 58, relax at the Sky Observatory Park on the roof level, enjoy the infinity pool, aquatic gym, wellness club, bowling center, and exclusive penthouses with breathtaking views. The flexible 80-month payment plan with just 1% monthly payments makes this prestigious address accessible to investors and homebuyers alike.
