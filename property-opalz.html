About Opalz

Welcome to Opalz by Danube - a stunning completed residential development in the thriving Dubai Silicon Oasis. This modern tower offers ready-to-move-in apartments with exceptional amenities and contemporary design. Located in one of Dubai's most dynamic tech and business hubs, Opalz provides the perfect blend of urban convenience and comfortable living.

Opalz features beautifully designed apartments with premium finishes, modern interiors, and world-class recreational facilities including a stunning infinity pool, state-of-the-art gym, and extensive sports amenities. The project is fully completed and ready for immediate occupancy, offering an ideal opportunity for both investors and homeowners seeking move-in-ready residences in a prime Dubai location.
