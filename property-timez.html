About Timez

Presenting Timez by Danube Properties - where affordable luxury meets innovation in Dubai Silicon Oasis. This modern residential tower brings a revolutionary concept to Dubai real estate with convertible apartments that adapt to your growing needs. Located just 2 minutes from Central Park and Silicon Central Mall, 3 minutes from the Metro, and only 12 minutes from Burj Khalifa, Timez offers the perfect blend of accessibility and tranquility.

With 45+ world-class amenities including an infinity pool, aquatic gym, library, business center, prayer hall, kids facilities, and sports courts, Timez caters to every lifestyle need. The unique convertible apartment feature allows studios to transform into 1-bedroom units and 1-bedroom apartments into 2-bedroom spaces, providing flexibility for the future. Select apartments even feature private pools. The flexible 71-month payment plan with just 1% monthly payments and 0% interest makes your dream home accessible and affordable.
